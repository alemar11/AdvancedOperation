The following protocols are available globally.

Shadow protocol

  • _TypeErasedErrorFailable

    Since Swift doesn’t currently support the use of protocols with associated types as actual types, a “shadow protocol” is needed to support computed properites such as isFailed for operations conforming to FailableOperation.
